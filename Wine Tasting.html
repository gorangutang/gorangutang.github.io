Wine Tasting

My Love for Wine

My parents went to grad school in Northern California and began a lifelong passion with wine. I now share in that passion.

I led the University of Cape Town's wine society, arranging weekly tastings from winemakers in the Western Cape, and am studying for the L1 of the Master Sommelier exam

This past summer, while my fiance was in Napa interning at a winery, the two of us visited a number of the valley's top producers.
